The Opportunity

Not a Typical Listing, a Strategic Acquisition

Set on a rare 100 × 302 ft lot backing directly onto the Thornhill Club Golf Course, this newly built custom estate delivers scale, privacy, and architectural presence in one of Uplands’ most exclusive residential enclaves.

Opportunities of this nature are increasingly difficult to replicate. The combined cost of land acquisition, permits, design, approvals, construction, and carrying time for a residence of this scale and caliber, would likely exceed the current asking price.

For the right buyer, this is an opportunity to secure a newly built custom estate without the delays, uncertainties, and friction of building from scratch.

For buyers seeking scale, privacy, and permanence, this represents value through replacement logic, not just price per square foot.
